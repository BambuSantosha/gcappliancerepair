Who We Are

Family Owned & Operated

Paul Capone — Owner & Lead Technician

Meet Paul Capone — owner, lead technician, and the guy who actually shows up at your door.

Paul grew up around complex systems. With an electronic engineer for a father, he spent his childhood troubleshooting, diagnosing, and fixing things most people would simply throw away. That upbringing didn't just give him technical skill — it gave him a philosophy: understand the system, find the root cause, fix it right the first time.

That philosophy drives everything at Gold Country Appliance Repair. Paul services Whirlpool, LG, Samsung, GE, and KitchenAid, as well as premium brands including Bosch, Sub-Zero, Wolf, Miele, and Thermador. He stays current with evolving appliance technologies so your newer units get the same expert attention as the classics.

Every repair is treated like it's his own appliance on the line — because he knows what it means to a family when the refrigerator goes out or the dryer stops heating. He brings honest assessments, fair pricing, and reliable solutions directly to your doorstep.

Fully licensed and insured, Gold Country Appliance Repair proudly serves residents from Penn Valley to Rocklin and Roseville — the communities that make up this corner of Northern California that we're proud to call home.
