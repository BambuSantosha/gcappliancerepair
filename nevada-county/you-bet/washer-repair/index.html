Why is my washer not spinning in You Bet?
A washer that won't spin is often caused by an unbalanced load, a failed lid switch or door latch, a worn drive belt, or a bad motor coupling. Gold Country Appliance Repair diagnoses the cause and fixes it fast.
Can you repair front-load washers in You Bet?
Yes. We repair both front-load and top-load washing machines in You Bet. Call (530) 277-3536 for same-day or next-day service.
My washer won't drain — is that an easy fix in You Bet?
Often yes. A clogged pump filter, kinked drain hose, or failed drain pump are the most common causes. Most washer drain repairs are completed in a single visit.
